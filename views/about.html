The Foundation of Our Consultancy: 26+ Years of experience

Our founder, Robert “Rosho” Hopp, has dedicated nearly three decades to strengthening businesses throughout Europa and beyond. He has more than 200 successful consulting missions to his name, along with an immense network of knowledgeable, innovative, and multilingual consultants.

It is years of combined entrepreneurial experience and Rosho’s augmented leadership approach that enable us to create effective solutions and get measurable results for our client’s businesses.

We work with a diverse range of clients from freelancers to SMEs to the top 100 European corporations.

Large and small, all of them have been impacted to some extent by the current sanitary crisis and the resulting economic disaster.

Rosho and our team have proven over and again that smarter use of digital processes and related methodologies are a viable solution. Supporting businesses in becoming lean and agile, adapting swiftly to change, and embracing augmentation is at the core of what we do. Specifically, we excel at using our extensive language skills and cultural openness to assist expat businesses in Germany, which are often overlooked.
